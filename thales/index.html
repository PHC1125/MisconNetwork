W — Water as Universal Principle

Thales proposed water as the fundamental substance of the cosmos. Plants growing near water, irrigation boosting yields, and seeds germinating with water all supported water as the primary material.

M — Moisture & Life

Moisture was not just useful — it was believed to BE the essence of life. A wilting plant was losing its fundamental substance, not merely a transport fluid.

S — Seeds & Germination

The dramatic transformation of a dry seed into a living plant when watered seemed to prove that water was directly converted into living matter and mass.

Integration

Modern science separates water's roles: as a reactant in photosynthesis, as a carrier of minerals, and as a cooling agent — but water alone is not the bulk source of plant mass.
